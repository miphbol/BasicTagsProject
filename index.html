Khemmis - Deceiver: A Super-Duper Quick Review

Release: 2021, Genre: Doom Metal

Deceiver is American doom metal outfit Khemmis' fifth and most recent album. Along with other bands such as Pallbearer, they are paving the way for the fusion of doom and traditional heavy metal. With each release, they are steadily progressing their sound and crafting very memorable music.
